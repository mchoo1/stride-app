# FitLife App — Product Brief \& Build Prompt## OverviewA community-powered nutrition and fitness platform connecting users, service providers, and real-world food data. Users track calories, get smart meal recommendations, and tap into a crowd-verified food database — all linked to wearables, local restaurants, delivery apps, and wellness professionals.\---## Core MissionHelp users achieve their health goals by making calorie tracking effortless, meal decisions smarter, and fitness knowledge shareable — powered by a real-world community database and an ecosystem of onboarded service providers.\---## 3 User Types### 1\. End Users (Consumers)* Track daily food and activity* Get macro-matched meal recommendations* Share and discover community content* Book classes or professional consultations### 2\. Service Providers* Gyms, wellness studios, restaurants, supermarkets, delivery apps* Upload menus with calorie and macro data* Publish classes, schedules, and promotions* Reach users actively aligned with their offerings### 3\. Professionals (Nutritionists \& PTs)* Offer paid plans and live sessions in-app* Build a follower base and content library* Verify and contribute to the community database\---## 6 Product Pillars|Pillar|Description||-|-||**Track**|Calories, macros, activity via photo logging and wearable sync||**Eat Smart**|AI-powered meal recommendations from restaurants, delivery, and groceries||**Food Database**|Community-sourced, crowd-verified macros with confidence scores||**Community**|Share workouts, meals, restaurants; archive favourites||**Providers**|Partner portal for gyms, restaurants, delivery apps, wellness pros||**Insights**|Energy balance dashboard, trends, AI nudges, goal tracking|\---## 12 Key Features### Core1. **Photo Calorie Scan** — Snap a meal → AI estimates calories/macros → matched against community DB for confirmation2. **Wearable Sync** — Fitbit, Apple Watch, Garmin, Google Fit; pulls steps, heart rate, active calories into daily energy balance3. **Goal-Based Plans** — Set goal (weight loss / muscle gain / maintenance) → auto-calculated macro targets, recalibrated weekly4. **Energy Balance Dashboard** — Real-time calories in vs. out, macro rings, streak tracking, exportable PDF summary### Differentiators5. **Community Macro Database** — User-submitted macros for restaurant dishes and grocery items; upvote system + confidence score + cross-reference with verified sources6. **Smart Meal Recommendations** — Remaining daily macros → surface nearby restaurant dishes, delivery items, or grocery products; filter by cuisine, budget, delivery app### Community7. **Community Feed** — Share meal logs, workout plans, lessons, restaurant finds; follow friends and creators8. **Workout Sharing \& Library** — Post full workout plans and class recommendations with estimated calorie burn; community rates and archives them9. **Personal Archive** — Save any community post (meals, workouts, restaurants) to a personal vault organised into custom folders; re-log saved meals in one tap### Revenue / Marketplace10. **Service Provider Portal** — Partner dashboard for gyms, restaurants, supermarkets, delivery apps to publish menus, class schedules, calorie data, and promotions11. **Delivery App Integration** — Deep-link to GrabFood, Deliveroo, Uber Eats pre-filtered to goal-matching dishes; show calories and macros alongside price before ordering12. **Nutritionist \& PT Booking** — Onboarded professionals offer 1:1 consultations, custom plans, and live classes directly in-app\---## Build Phases### Phase 1 — MVP (Track \& Eat)* Photo calorie scan* Manual food logging* Wearable sync (Apple Watch + Fitbit)* Goal setup and macro target calculator* Energy balance dashboard### Phase 2 — Growth (Community \& Providers)* Community macro database with confidence scoring* Smart meal recommendations engine* Community feed with save/archive* Workout sharing library* Service provider onboarding portal### Phase 3 — Monetise (Marketplace)* Delivery app deep-links (Grab, Deliveroo, Uber Eats)* PT and nutritionist booking and payments* Sponsored provider listings* Premium user subscription tier* Partner API access\---## Tech Considerations* **Image Recognition**: Vision AI (Google Vision API or custom model) for photo calorie estimation* **Wearable APIs**: Apple HealthKit, Fitbit Web API, Garmin Connect IQ, Google Fit REST API* **Database**: Hybrid of USDA/Open Food Facts verified data + community submissions* **Recommendations**: Macro-remaining algorithm + geolocation for nearby providers* **Community Trust**: Upvote weighting + professional verification badges + confidence score per entry* **Provider Portal**: Separate web dashboard with menu/calorie data CMS* **Monetisation**: Freemium (free tracking, paid recommendations + booking), provider subscription, affiliate delivery commissions\---## Cowork Build Prompt> See Section below
