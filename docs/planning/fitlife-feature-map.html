02

Feature Map

Photo Calorie Scan
Snap a meal photo → AI estimates calories and macros instantly. Suggests closest matches from the community database for user confirmation, with a confidence score.
Core
Wearable Sync
Two-way integration with Fitbit, Apple Watch, Garmin, and Google Fit. Pulls steps, heart rate, and active calories directly into daily energy balance.
Core
Goal-Based Plans
Set a goal — weight loss, muscle gain, or maintenance. App auto-calculates daily macro targets using BMR, adjusts as activity data comes in, and recalibrates weekly.
Core
Energy Balance Dashboard
Real-time calories in vs. out view. Weekly trend charts, macro breakdown rings, streak tracking, and exportable PDF summary. improved
Core
Community Macro Database
Users submit macros for restaurant dishes and grocery items. Upvote system + professional cross-reference builds a confidence score per entry — going far beyond standard food labels. improved
Differentiator
Smart Meal Recommendations
Based on remaining daily macros, surfaces nearby restaurant dishes, delivery items, or grocery products that fit. Filter by cuisine, budget, and delivery app. improved
Differentiator
Community Feed
Users share meal logs, workout plans, class recommendations, and restaurant finds. Follow friends or creators. Save and archive any post for personal reuse.
Community
Workout Sharing & Library
Post full workout plans, class recommendations, or PT session notes. Community rates and archives them. Each plan includes estimated calorie burn per session. improved
Community
Personal Archive
Save any community post — meals, workouts, restaurant picks — to a personal vault. Organise into custom folders. Re-log saved meals in one tap.
Community
Service Provider Portal
Gyms, wellness studios, restaurants, supermarkets, and delivery apps get a partner dashboard to publish menus, class schedules, calorie data, and promotions. improved
Revenue
Delivery App Integration
Deep-link to GrabFood, Deliveroo, and Uber Eats pre-filtered to goal-matching dishes. Shows calorie and macro data alongside price before the user orders. new
Revenue
Nutritionist & PT Booking
Onboarded professionals offer 1:1 consultations, custom plans, and live classes directly in-app — integrated with the provider portal and Stripe payments. new
Revenue
