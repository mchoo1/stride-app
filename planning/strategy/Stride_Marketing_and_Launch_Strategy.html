1.Strategy at a Glance

Stride wins Singapore by being the first calorie/activity app that actually knows Singapore — FairPrice ready meals, 7-Eleven onigiri, Toast Box wraps, nearby gyms, parks and trails. We use that authenticity to earn creator-led word of mouth, then scale through App Store featuring and a tight freemium paywall.

Three commitments

  1. Singapore-native, not Singapore-translated. Every screen, screenshot and creator brief must feel unmistakably SG.
  2. Earn trust before chasing scale. Retention & reviews first; paid spend only after PMF signal.
  3. Decision over data. Lead every message with what users should do, not what we track.

2.Who We're Talking To

Primary audience — "Goal-Oriented Office Pro"

Persona A · Primary

Urban SG professional, 28–42

Owns a wearable, eats lunch out 4–5x/week, has tried MyFitnessPal and quit. Goal: lose 3–8kg or maintain post-30 metabolism. Time-poor, budget-aware, brand-conscious.

Persona B · Secondary

Active Parent 35–50

Wants to model healthy habits, needs "tell me what to grab" answers. Open to corporate wellness via employer.

Persona C · Growth

New-to-Fitness Beginner

First gym, intimidated by macros. Responds to friendly, plain-English framing (no jargon, no gamification).

Persona D · Niche

Returning-to-Fitness 40+

Cares about BF%, BMR, trend lines. Wants gentle nudges, not streak pressure.

Jobs to be done

  • When I'm deciding what to order for lunch, I want to know which nearby option fits my remaining calorie budget, so I can stay on track without thinking about it.
  • When I weigh myself on Sunday, I want a clear line showing trajectory toward my goal, so I can keep going without obsessing.
  • When I finish work with calories to burn, I want a nearby gym or park with opening hours, so I can move without planning.

3.Positioning & Brand Promise

Positioning statement

For health-aware Singaporeans who eat out and want to stay on track, Stride is the simplest one-stop health app that turns your daily calorie budget into clear answers — what to eat nearby, where to move, and how your body is trending. Unlike global trackers built on US food data, Stride is built on a verified Singapore food database and Google Places, so the first answer is always the right answer for here.

Brand promise

Move. Eat. Connect. — Stride makes the healthy choice the easy choice, in your neighbourhood, in your language, in 3 seconds.

Category we're carving out

Not "another calorie tracker." Stride is the decision layer between your goals, your food, and your neighbourhood.

5.Brand Identity & Tone

Voice

  • Clear, friendly, direct. Talks like a knowledgeable friend — not a coach.
  • Plain English. Explains the "why" in one sentence.
  • Calm confidence — no hype, no guilt-tripping.

Visual direction

  • Clean, generous whitespace, one hero number per screen.
  • Palette: deep navy (#0F1B2D), calm green (#1E7F5C) for "on track", warm amber (#F2A93B) for attention.
  • Photography: real SG food (hawker, kopitiam, convenience), real SG neighbourhoods (HDB, CBD, parks). No generic stock.

Tagline options

  • Primary: Move. Eat. Connect.
  • Alt 1: Singapore's simplest health companion.
  • Alt 2: Eat smart. Move nearby. Stride.

Brief (every creator gets the same one-pager)

9.Product Launch — Overall Approach

Sequenced launch: closed beta → open waitlist → public launch → creator wave → paid pilot. Each stage has a go/no-go gate; we don't throw marketing spend at a leaky bucket.

Stage 1
Closed Beta
  • 150 invited users
  • Weekly retention readout
  • Gate: D3 ≥70%
Stage 2
Open Waitlist
  • Landing page + IG + TikTok teaser
  • Target 1,500 signups
  • Gate: ≥500 waitlist
Stage 3
Public Launch
  • App Store + Play live (SG)
  • PR + creator wave
  • Gate: ≥4.5 rating in 72h
Stage 4
Scale
  • Paid pilot (S$5k)
  • Stride+ paywall live
  • Gate: CAC ≤ S$8, D7 ≥ 25%

Launch principles

  1. Ship small, land hard. Better to launch with 4 rock-solid pillars than 8 half-built ones.
  2. One market, one language. SG-only messaging — no dilution, no geo-split testing.
  3. Reviews are oxygen. Aggressive in-app review prompts at moments of success (scan worked, weekly PDF generated, weight trend green).
  4. Every user ≥ S$10 of value. Optimise first-week experience like it's the only thing that matters — because it is.

10.12-Week Launch Countdown

Calendar runs T-12 → T-0, where T-0 is the public App Store / Play release day in SG.

T-12 / T-11
Foundation. Lock v2.0 spec. Finalise design system. Set up App Store Connect & Google Play Console. Stand up analytics (Mixpanel / Firebase). Begin landing page build. Draft PDPA privacy policy.
T-10 / T-9
Build momentum. Ship core MVP builds. Wire Apple Health + Fitbit. Seed database. Begin creator long-list (30 targets) and outreach. Landing page live with waitlist.
T-8
Content starts. Begin 8-week content calendar (Pillar 1–4). Teaser reels on IG + TikTok. Invite internal dogfood group (10–15 users). First Reddit soft-mention as founder.
T-7 / T-6
Closed beta. Open TestFlight + Play closed beta — 150 invitees. Weekly cohort retention readouts. Fix top-3 friction each week. Sign 4–6 creators with launch dates in T-2 / T-1.
T-5 / T-4
Polish + press. Beta v2 with fixes. First press outreach (TIA, e27, CNA Lifestyle) under embargo. Open waitlist to public — target 500+. Prepare App Store assets (screenshots, subtitle, keywords).
T-3
Submission window opens. App Store + Play submissions. Age rating, App Privacy form, Google Data safety form. Finalise creator briefs. Lock media embargo dates.
T-2
Go/no-go review. Retention gate: ≥70% D3 on closed beta. Crash-free ≥99.5%. Final rehearsal of day-of runbook. Creators get final assets + links.
T-1
Priming. Countdown posts across IG/TikTok. Waitlist email: "We're live Tuesday." Reddit & Telegram group founder post scheduled. Team on standby for day-of monitoring.
T-0
Launch day. Live on App Store + Play (SG). Creator wave goes live within a 12-hour window. PR embargo lifts 9am SGT. Founder posts long-form story on LinkedIn + IG. Founder-led Reddit AMA 8pm SGT.

12.Post-Launch — First 30 / 60 / 90 Days

First 30 days

Harden & learn

Weekly cohort retention. Ship 2 hotfix releases. Measure which content pillar drove the best installs. Double down on winner, kill the laggard.

Days 31–60

Turn on monetisation

Stride+ paywall live once D7 ≥25%. Paid pilot with S$5k across Meta + TikTok. Begin App Store featuring pitch.

Days 61–90

Partner wave

Announce first 2–3 anchor partners (Founding Partner badge). Launch referral program. Begin corporate wellness outreach.

Weekly business review (every Monday)

  • MAU, DAU, D1/D7/D30 retention by cohort.
  • Installs by source (creator code, organic, paid, ASO).
  • Top 5 scan misses this week → input into AI retraining queue.
  • App Store rating + top 10 reviews — respond to all 1-/2-star reviews.
  • Stride+ conversion funnel once live.
