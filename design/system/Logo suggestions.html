Direction A · S-Stride + Anton
It's the only mark that reads at 16px in a phone header and still has personality at 1024px. The cursive S doubles as both the wordmark initial and a motion cue (stride = step forward). Anton is already in the system. Lowest friction, strongest identity. Runner-up: B · Leaf on navy for a quieter, more premium feel — consider as a "pro" / watch-face variant.
