## 1. Who we are

Stride ("we", "us") is operated by **[LEGAL ENTITY NAME / SOLE PROPRIETOR]**
([UEN if registered]), Singapore. This policy explains how we collect, use, and
protect your personal data in line with Singapore's **Personal Data Protection
Act 2012 (PDPA)**.

**Data Protection Officer (DPO):** [NAME] — **[dpo@yourdomain.com]**

## 2. What we collect

**You give us:**
- Account: name, email address (via Firebase Authentication).
- Profile: gender, age, height, current & target weight, fitness goal, activity
  level, dietary preferences/flags.
- Activity you log: food entries, meals, water, weight history, workouts/activity.
- Food photos you choose to scan.
- Feedback and meal corrections you submit.

**Collected automatically:**
- Approximate/precise **location (GPS)** — only when you enable it, to show
  nearby food outlets and gyms.
- Basic device/usage and log data, and cookies/local storage needed to run the app.

We do **not** intentionally collect data from anyone under 18. Stride is intended
for adults; if you are under 18, please use it only with a parent/guardian.

## 3. Why we use it (purposes)

- To create and run your account and show your dashboard.
- To calculate calorie/macro targets and personalised recommendations.
- To show nearby outlets and food options (location).
- To analyse food photos you scan (estimate the food and its nutrition).
- To improve the food database and the app, including community corrections.
- To contact you about your account or important service notices.

We rely on your **consent** (which you give by creating an account and using the
relevant features) and, where applicable, our legitimate interests in operating
and improving the service. You can withdraw consent at any time (see Section 7).

## 4. Who we share it with (service providers)

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited data with trusted providers
who process it on our behalf, including:
- **Google / Firebase** — authentication, database, and hosting.
- **Vercel** — application hosting.
- **Google Maps/Places** — nearby-place search (receives location queries).
- **Anthropic** — processes food photos you scan to estimate nutrition.
- **USDA FoodData Central / HPB sources** — nutrition lookups (no personal data sent).

## 5. Overseas transfer

Some of these providers process data **outside Singapore** (e.g. the United
States). Where we transfer your personal data overseas, we take steps to ensure
it is protected to a standard comparable to the PDPA, including through the
providers' contractual data-protection commitments.

## 6. How long we keep it

We keep your personal data for as long as your account is active and as needed
for the purposes above, then delete or anonymise it within a reasonable period,
unless a longer period is required by law.

## 7. Your rights (PDPA)

You may, at any time:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you and ask how it has been used.
- **Correct** inaccurate or incomplete data (much of this you can edit in-app).
- **Withdraw consent** and request deletion of your account and data.

To exercise any of these, contact our DPO at **[dpo@yourdomain.com]**. We will
respond within the timeframes required by the PDPA.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted connections, access controls via
Firebase) to protect your data. No system is perfectly secure, but we take
reasonable steps to safeguard it and will notify you and the PDPC of any
notifiable data breach as required by law.

## 9. Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new version here with a revised
"last updated" date and, for significant changes, notify you in-app.

## 10. Contact

Questions or requests: **[dpo@yourdomain.com]**.
